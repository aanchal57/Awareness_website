About The Organization

Our Mission

Menstruation and its consequent needs have been a topic largely kept under wraps in Indian society. There remains a large and persistent gap between access to resources and information regarding menstruation by women in India.

Our vision is to create a sustained space that includes educating, empowering, and engaging women in Indian society, concerning menstruation. We aim to achieve menstrual equity and reduce the stigma surrounding periods by educating people and break menstruation barriers so girls be retained in school to get lifetime skills and control their lives.

Still in India women menstruating are considered as impure, sinful and even dirty. Many elements come at play in perpetuating this misconception. As aforementioned, patriarchal societies reinforce period shaming. Besides, the cultural and religious ideologies embedded in Indians daily lives also contribute in standardizing the stigma around periods.

Menstruation stigma has a huge impact on girls' education. They often are forced to skip school, missing days at school can lead girls to drop out, putting them at greater risk. Myths make it harder for women to talk about their period which also leads to the silence, shame and misconceptions. So, letâ€™s spread the word and debunk all the myths surrounding periods. We achieve so by our three pillars of belief: Educate, Emancipate and Elevate.

Educate

Educating and creating awareness about the misconceptions and taboos concerning menstruation.

Emancipate

Emancipating all menstruators from the societal limitations which are a hindrance to their growth.

Elevate

Elevating the mindset of society and empowering all the menstruators out there.
